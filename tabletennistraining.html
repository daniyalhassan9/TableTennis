About the Website

The primary vision for this website is to document and pass on all my experiences as Table Tennis player/coach. I have been playing table tennis at a club level for almost ten years now, and I've learned a lot about the sport. I've been fortunate enough to be a student of some of the best players in northeast area, and I've also have been coached by the Australian Men's team head coach Brett Clarke. I've gained a lot of knowledge by working with these coaches, and I feel an obligation to pass what I've learned about the great sport of Table Tennis to people who are just starting out in their journey, and help them avoid the mistakes that I've made.

This website will be primarily focuesd on the following things

  • Booking a Lesson
  • Blade Reviews
  • Rubber Review
